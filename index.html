About Me:

My Work:

I have extensive experience in research focused positions in psychology labs that worked in the field. I have been involved in data collection initiatives with these research labs. I also enjoy taking the time to take up volunteering positions in the mental health sector. My past experiences include; play leader at an Austin based therapy facility, and volunteering experience at a private practice. I am currently looking to expand my knowledge and skills by learning web development.

My Education:

I graduated with my bachelor's degree in psychology from the University of Texas at Austin, with a minor in Educational Psychology. I took classes related to mental health, positive psychology and child, adolescent and adult development.

My Hobbies:

My hobbies include touring the city I live in, watching TV, playing around with websites, blogging and poetry. I enjoy exploring topics that relate to gender issues and social justice. When I'm not working, you can find me cozied up in my room with a book or a good TV show
